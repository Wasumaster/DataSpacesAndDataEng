=== ENGINEERING INTERPRETATION ===
1. Sentinel-2 (Optical) is heavily preferred for vegetation (NDVI) and visual interpretation under clear, daylight conditions.
2. Sentinel-2 becomes unreliable under cloudy conditions and is completely blind during night-time passes (requires solar illumination).
3. Sentinel-1 SAR (Synthetic Aperture Radar) is prioritized during cloudy weather and night operations because microwave pulses penetrate clouds and do not rely on the sun.
4. Federated observation selection significantly improves mission robustness by guaranteeing continuous data flow regardless of local weather and planetary rotation.
